Before Volition released the full version of FreeSpace 2, they released a short demo with two single player missions. Although made by Volition, the canonicity of the demo is disputed, but it is quite a good prelude to the actual game. The timeframe for these two missions is somewhere between A Lion at the Door and Straight, No Chaser.



Description

Thirty-two years have passed since the Lucifer was destroyed. Its reactors were breached causing it go nova as it exited the SOL jump node. Since then the Earth has been cutoff from the rest of the galaxy. A group of rebels, the Neo Terran Front (NTF), have been putting a huge strain on the Terran-Vasudan relations and the new Galactic Terran-Vasudan Alliance (GTVA). Due to a discovery made by the NTF, an ancient device created by the "Ancients," a portal has been opened leading directly into Shivan space. The Shivans from "The Great War" are back and they are pissed off. You, a member of the human "Lost Generation", will have to answer the call to war.
